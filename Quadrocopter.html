Quardocopter

This is a project I undertook with a team of friends back in grade 10, it was our 2011 entry for the Robofest robotics competition hosted by Lawrence Technological University.

The project consisted of a flying robotic quadrotor platform made from parts I could find at Homedepot and the local hobby stores.
